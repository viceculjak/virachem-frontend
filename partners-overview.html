Partner Tiers

ViraChem works with qualified partners under a tiered access framework. Partner capabilities expand over time based on performance, compliance, and demonstrated demand.

Tier 1

Referral Partner

Introduce qualified leads. RUO only. Step back. No power. No risk.

Example: A consultant introduces a university lab; ViraChem handles everything; the consultant is compensated for the referral.

Tier 2

Authorized Access Partner

Main working partner tier. Regional front-end: manage client relationships, collect specs and timelines, coordinate projects, discuss schedules and capacity, forecast volumes (non-binding or semi-binding), add service markup.

Can do:

  • Act as regional front-end
  • Manage client relationships
  • Collect specs & timelines
  • Coordinate projects
  • Discuss schedules and capacity
  • Forecast volumes (non-binding or semi-binding)
  • Add service markup

Cannot do:

  • Hold stock
  • Speculate
  • Relabel
  • Sell off-the-shelf
  • Make claims
  • Bypass ViraChem

Manufacturing and documentation remain under ViraChem control. No stock. No grey market.

Tier 3

Authorized Distributor (Stock-Holding)

The only tier with inventory. Reached after sustained performance as Tier 2: real volume, clean compliance, accurate forecasting. Not offered to partners who ask for stock early.

Can do:

  • Hold approved SKUs only
  • Fixed vial formats
  • Fixed RUO labeling
  • Supply pre-qualified institutional clients
  • Shorten lead times regionally

Cannot do:

  • Custom manufacturing
  • Relabel freely
  • Sub-distribute
  • Make claims
  • Mix cosmetic and RUO

Geography: One distributor per region where demand justifies stock (e.g. DACH, Nordics, Benelux), not one per country. Logistics, not freedom.
